  • How do I get started with PayGate API?

    To get started, complete your business verification, generate your API keys in the Settings page, and follow our integration guide in the documentation.

  • What payment methods are supported?

    PayGate API currently supports airtime top-up, data bundles, electricity bill payments, and cable TV subscriptions for all major providers in Nigeria.

  • How long does it take to process a transaction?

    Most transactions are processed in real-time, typically within 5-10 seconds. During peak periods or network issues, it may take up to 2 minutes.

  • What happens if a transaction fails?

    If a transaction fails, the system will attempt to reverse it automatically. You will receive a notification with the status and reason for failure. Funds will be returned to your account balance.

  • How do I handle webhook notifications?

    Set up a webhook endpoint in your application to receive real-time notifications. Configure the webhook URL in your PayGate dashboard under Settings > Webhooks.
